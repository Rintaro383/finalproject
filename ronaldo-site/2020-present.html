Chapter 5

2020 – Present

Still scoring, still the best.

Back to Manchester United

In 2021, Ronaldo left Juventus and returned to Manchester United. He scored 27 goals in his return season.

However the team did not perform well overall. In 2022, he left United by mutual agreement.

Ronaldo became the all-time top international goal scorer in 2021, surpassing Ali Daei's record of 109 goals for Iran.

International Record

Al Nassr — Saudi Arabia

In January 2023, Ronaldo joined Al Nassr in Saudi Arabia. He kept scoring goals at a high level.

As of April 2026, he has scored 969 career goals — the most in football history.
